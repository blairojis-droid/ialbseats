Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how ialbseats collects, uses, and shares personal information when you use our services. By using our website and services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain our services, including sourcing tickets and sending quotes.
  • Communicate with you about requests, options, orders, and support.

  • Improve and customise our services and website.

Information Sharing

  • We may share your information with third-party service providers essential to our operations (e.g., payment processors, ticketing/transfer platforms, hosting/form providers).
  • We may disclose information to comply with legal obligations or enforce our terms.

Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to help protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we aim to safeguard data appropriately.

Your Choices

You can contact us to access, correct, or delete your personal data, subject to applicable law. You can also ask us to stop using your information for non-essential communications.

Cookies

We may use cookies or similar technologies to operate the site and improve your experience. You can control cookies via your browser settings.

Retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Please review it periodically for changes.
