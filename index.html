FAQ

How do you deliver tickets?
Most tickets are delivered via secure mobile transfer or e-tickets sent directly to your email. Full instructions are provided with your order confirmation.
Do you serve international buyers?
Absolutely — we regularly assist international clients and provide clear delivery instructions. We serve buyers worldwide, with a strong focus on UK, Spain, Germany, and US events.
How quickly can you find last-minute tickets?
Often the same day. Our average response time is under 1 hour. For truly last-minute requests, contact us directly via WhatsApp for the fastest response.
Do you handle group orders?
Yes — we can source adjacent or nearby seats for groups of any size. Just mention the group size and seating preference in your request.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and PayPal. Payment details are shared after you confirm your quote. We never ask for payment before sending a confirmed quote.
