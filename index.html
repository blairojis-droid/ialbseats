Frequently Asked Questions

How do you deliver tickets?
Most tickets are delivered via secure mobile transfer or e-tickets. Instructions are provided with your order.
Are mobile tickets accepted everywhere?
Yes. Most venues support mobile tickets. We’ll review delivery options before you confirm.
Is payment safe and secure?
Yes. We use trusted payment providers and never store full card details.
What if my event is canceled?
If your event is canceled and not rescheduled, you’ll receive a full refund.
How quickly can you find last-minute tickets?
Often the same day. Tell us your timing and we’ll prioritize accordingly.
Do you handle group orders?
Yes, we can source adjacent or nearby seats for groups. Add this in your request.
Do you serve international buyers?
Absolutely — we regularly assist international clients and provide clear delivery instructions.
How do quotes work?
Tell us event, city/date, and quantity. We’ll send options with pricing and delivery details. You confirm before paying.
